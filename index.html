About Us

Legible Ledgers Bookkeeping was founded by Marjorie Arnold after 10 years as a Full Charge Bookkeeper. The company helps small businesses focus on their core operations by outsourcing bookkeeping and payroll tasks.

With a skilled and trustworthy team, Legible Ledgers ensures accuracy, timeliness, and peace of mind, while also educating clients and giving them a clear view of their business performance. The goal is to establish reliable accounting practices that provide control and confidence both now and in the future.

We empower our clients by magnifying their results through clarity.
